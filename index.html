About the Author

Behind the Pen

I am an independent author fascinated by the complexities of the human condition. Writing isn't just a hobby; it's a way of documenting the invisible threads that connect us.

When I am not writing literary fiction, I am exploring other genres and formats. As a self-publisher, I believe in the freedom to tell stories exactly how they wish to be told.

- Yashasvi Aditya

Baa

A literary fiction short story

Baa is a quiet village story from the hills of Himachal Pradesh, following an elderly woman shaped by simple, steady routines. It explores companionship, aging and a gentle, enduring form of grief and courage.
Written in calm, understated prose, it’s a short reflective read meant to be taken slowly.
